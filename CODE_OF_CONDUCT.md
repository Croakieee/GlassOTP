# Code of Conduct

## My Approach

This project is maintained by a single developer, and I aim to keep it open, respectful, and constructive for anyone who wants to contribute or interact.

## Expected Behavior

* Be respectful and polite
* Provide constructive feedback
* Stay on topic in discussions

## Unacceptable Behavior

* Harassment, insults, or toxic behavior
* Discrimination of any kind
* Spam or irrelevant content

## Reporting Issues

If you experience or notice unacceptable behavior, please contact me privately instead of creating a public issue.

## Enforcement

I reserve the right to remove comments, close discussions, or block users who violate these guidelines.

## Final Notes

This is a personal project, but contributions and respectful collaboration are always welcome.
